About Hoop Yoga

Even if you haven't picked up a hula hoop for years, you will be able to hoop!

Gloria will focus on grounding and energising your body, with detailed instructions on controlling the hoop. We use the hoop on all parts of the body, providing a great workout, cleaning and toning session.

You will be given foundations and techniques so you can work at your own pace, at any level - beginner or advanced.

Most importantly, hooping is a fun! Stepping into the hoop will make you feel like a child again. The hoops provided in class are not like the ones you played with as a kid - they are custom made, durable and hand-crafted for adults to exercise and dance with.
