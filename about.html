About Hoop Yoga

Hoop yoga is a unique form of movement meditation which is practiced to music. Technical guidance on yoga postures (asanas) will prepare the body for hooping moves while the flowing exercises (vinyasas) and the free flow dance session will provide a great cardiovascular workout. Breath control (pranayama) will also let you switch off the mind and connect with your body

Most importantly, hoop yoga is FUN! It's a great way to exercise. Stepping into the hoop will make you feel like a child again. The hoops provided in class are not like the ones you played with as a kid - they are custom made, durable and hand-crafted for adults to exercise and dance with.

We encourage movement which feels good. Advice is provided on controlling the hoop and safely practicing yoga, but these classes are also about exploring and letting go. Come and give it a whirl!

Key benefits of hoop yoga are:

  • stress relief and letting go
  • core strengthening and toning
  • cardiovascular fitness
  • increases flexibility
  • complete workout for the body and mind
