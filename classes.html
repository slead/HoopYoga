Classes

Classes are energetic and suitable for all levels, even if you've never picked up a hoop or been on a yoga mat.

Clear instructions are given on both yoga sequences and hooping drills so you can work at your own pace, at any level.

Gloria will take you through a series of moves that engage the body. You are urged to listen to your body and advance according to how you feel that day. These classes are about feeling good whilst progressing with your yoga and hooping technique. We encourage you to be creative within the hoop and develop your own style and expression in hoop dance.

Hoop:Yoga sessions are structured into a 6 week course. We will focus on different yoga and hooping aspects each week so we can progress and concentrate on various parts of the body.

Hoops and yoga mats are provided, or you can BYO.

Drop-ins are always welcome - please email to ensure places are available.
