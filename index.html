What is Hoop Yoga?

Hoop Yoga is about exploring the dynamics, functions and benefits of hooping and yoga.

The concept of balance, grounding, energy and meditation apply to both forms of exercise - yoga and hooping - and our dedication is to expand knowledge as it relates to yogic philosophy, practice, and contemporary hoop dance.

Yoga and hooping are so good together! Hooping definitely needs support of a regular discipline like yoga, to strengthen, stretch and protect the body and similarly, yoga can benefit from adding a hoop to provide a tool for alignment, explore poses in a fun way and to activate your energy.

Yoga and hooping will help you let go and relax more. It is our aim to make yoga and hooping accessible to everyone and cater to individual needs. All classes are taught one-on-one or in small group settings so you get the best attention, as every body, shape, size and ability is different and unique - just like you.
