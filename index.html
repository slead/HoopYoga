About Hoop Yoga

Hoop yoga is a unique form of movement meditation which is practiced to music. Technical guidance on yoga postures (asanas) will prepare the body for hooping moves while the flowing exercises (vinyasas) and hoop dance session will provide a great cardiovascular workout. Breath control (pranayama) will also let you switch off the mind and connect with your body

Most importantly, hoop yoga is a fun way to exercise. Stepping into the hoop will make you feel like a child again. The hoops provided in class are not like the ones you played with as a kid - they are custom made, durable and hand-crafted for adults to exercise and dance with.

We encourage movement which feels good. Advice is provided on controlling the hoop and safely practicing yoga, but these classes are also about letting go. Come and give it a whirl!

Key benefits of hoop yoga are:

  • stress relief
  • core strengthening
  • body conditioning
  • toning arms, legs, waist and hips
  • cardiovascular fitness
  • increased flexibility
  • complete workout for the body and mind

Hoop Yoga Classes

Gloria will take you through a series of uplifting stretches and moves that engage the whole body. These classes are about feeling good whilst progressing with your yoga and hooping technique so we encourage you to be creative within the hoop and express your own style and movement. The hoop is such a great tool for alignment and posture, and you'll find many creative ways to work within your circle.

Hoop Yoga sessions are structured into workshop classes focusing on various parts of the body. We will focus on different yoga and hooping aspects each week so we can progress and build up our stamina controlling the hoop.

Hoops and yoga mats are provided, or you can BYO.

Drop-ins are always welcome - please email to ensure places are available.
